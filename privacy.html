MacroTracker

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Overview

MacroTracker is a food diary and health-tracking app for logging nutrition, water, weight, and activity. We respect your privacy and collect only what is needed to make the app work. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it.

Data We Collect

When you use MacroTracker, we collect and store:

  • Account information (email, username, name) — provided when you register with an email address or use Sign in with Apple.
  • Nutrition and food diary entries (meals, macronutrient values, water intake, body-composition figures, daily goals).
  • Food photos you add to a meal, which are processed to estimate the meal's contents (see AI Meal Recognition).
  • Apple Health (HealthKit) data such as steps, heart rate, sleep, and workouts — only if you grant permission (see Apple Health).
  • App preferences (theme, language, daily goals).
  • A push notification token, if you enable reminders, used only to deliver notifications.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the app's functionality:

  • Authenticate and secure your account.
  • Store and display your food diary and health metrics.
  • Recognize meals from photos and text.
  • Sync your data across your devices and send reminders you have enabled.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

MacroTracker reads data from Apple Health only after you explicitly grant permission. This data is used only to display and track your health and fitness metrics inside the app. We never use Apple Health data for advertising or marketing, and we never share it with third parties for advertising or data-mining purposes. You can review or revoke access at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.

AI Meal Recognition

When you submit a food photo or a text description of a meal, it is sent to our servers and then to Anthropic (the Claude API) to identify the food and estimate its nutritional values. Anthropic processes this content only to return a result to us and, under its API terms, does not use it to train its models. We share with Anthropic only the meal content you submit — not your account credentials or Apple Health data.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text. All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data, and we never use it for advertising, analytics, or user tracking. We share limited data only with the service providers listed below, strictly to operate the app.

Third-Party Services

MacroTracker relies on a small set of providers that process data on our behalf:

  • Anthropic — AI-based meal recognition from photos and text.
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple (authentication).
  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — delivery of push notifications (device token only).

These providers act as data processors and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes. MacroTracker does not integrate any advertising, analytics, or user-tracking services.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app or by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

MacroTracker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page and reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@healthtracker.vip.
